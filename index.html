--- layout: home title: ATOMIUM subtitle: ALMA tracing the origins of molecules forming dust in oxygen-rich M-type stars --- ATOMIUM is an accepted ALMA large program (ALMA id: 2018.1.00659.L) aiming at studying the wind of cool O-rich evolved stars. The project will also make use of other instruments and tools such as VLT/SPHERE, VLTI/MATISSE, MERCATOR/HERMES, radiative transfer models, chemical networks, binary model.

PI: Leen Decin (KU Leuven, Belgium)
CoPI: Carl Gottlieb (Harvard-Smithsonian Center for Astrophysics, United States)
